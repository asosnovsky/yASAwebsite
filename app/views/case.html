Case Competition

The Goal

Over the last few years several institutions such as Munich and the Actuarial Student National Association, have built various competitions that provides the chance to student Actuaries to show off their abilities to research, analyze and present. This year, York University will be constructing a competition similar to that of these instructions. The goal is to test and appraise the abilities of the York student body. It will also serve as the starting grounds of a York Actuarial Competitive team.

The Time

For the upcoming year of 2015/16, we are actively planning four actuarial case contests within York University, one each term (F/W). The first contest will be held on November-6th 2015. The second will be held sometime in the winter term.

The Setup

Contestants will be able to work in teams or by themselves. Each team will be given a case which they will have to provide a solution for. The cases will be given to competitors two week in advance. Where they will be able to research and prepare a presentation in which they will have to propose a solution.

The Ruling

Contestants will have 10 minutes to present, where they will be judged on their analysis, presentation, and research skills. Each category will be awarded a score of 0-9, and then will be averaged out to a final score.

The Event

There will be one competition each semester;

1. Fall Competition,

2. Winter Competition.

The Judges

The panel will consist of industry professionals, including Professor, Actuaries, Insurance Analysts and others.

The Students

Any student who is enrolled at York University can participate. If students choose to work in groups, the group will have a maximum of 3 people.

What is an Actuary?

An actuary is a business professional who analyzes the financial consequences of risk. They evaluate the likelihood of uncertain future events, design creative ways to reduce their likelihood..

  • Mathematics

    An Actuary must be very quick and capable in devising mathematical proofs and techniques to solve problems.

  • Economics

    Economic theory plays a major role in acturarial science, as many economical models play a role in decision making.

  • Risk and Insurance

    In order to deal with risky choices such as loans, debt and insirance, companies employ acturay to estimate the future costs and risk of such transactions.
